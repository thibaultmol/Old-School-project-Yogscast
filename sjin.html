Paul Sykes, under the username Sjin (pronounced as Shin or sometimes Sa-jin) is a member of the Yogscast whose job is the creation of maps in Minecraft. On the 10th of July 2012, Sjin appeared for a short while in a video in a news article about the Yogscast on the BBC website, talking about his role in the Yogscast. This was the first time Sjin was seen in real life.
